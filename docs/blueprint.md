# **App Name**: Legal Compass

## Core Features:

- Law Lookup: Search and retrieve laws, statutes, and legal codes specific to a selected jurisdiction (state, county, municipality, or tribe) within the USA.
- Rights and Obligations: Clearly present an individual's rights and obligations under the selected law, written in plain language.
- Case Law Summaries: Provide concise summaries of relevant case law, explaining how judges have interpreted specific laws in the past.
- AI Legal Guidance Tool: A generative AI tool that answers user questions about legal topics and offers personalized guidance, based on the user's selected jurisdiction and legal issues. This tool will consider all the relevant information when making the guidance.
- Offline Access: Allow users to access previously viewed laws and case summaries offline.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark blue (#2E4A62) to evoke trust, authority, and the established legal system.
- Background color: Light gray (#EAEAEA), a desaturated tone of the primary hue, provides a clean and neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Gold (#A98D6B), an analogous color to the primary hue, provides highlights.
- Body: 'Inter' sans-serif font to provide clear and accessible readability.
- Headlines: 'Space Grotesk' sans-serif font to offer clear hierachy for easier understanding.
- Use clear, universally recognizable icons to represent different legal categories (e.g., traffic laws, property rights) and actions (e.g., search, save, share).
- Prioritize a clean, intuitive layout with a prominent search bar and easy-to-navigate sections.
